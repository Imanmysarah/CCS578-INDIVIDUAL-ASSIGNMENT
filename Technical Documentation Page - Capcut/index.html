Introduction

CapCut is a powerful video editing application developed by ByteDance.

It's widely used for creating engaging short-form video content, particularly on platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

CapCut is available on mobile and desktop, offering both beginner-friendly and professional features.

It supports multiple video formats and provides an intuitive editing interface.

One of the standout features is its vast library of music, effects, and templates.

Download CapCut from the official app store.

With CapCut, users can trim, split, and merge clips seamlessly.

Its AI-powered features like auto captions and background removal add significant value.

CapCut is free to use, with some advanced features available via subscription.

capcut.edit(video).export('mp4')
Interface Overview

The CapCut interface is clean and easy to navigate.

The main workspace includes a timeline, preview window, and tool panel.

Users can drag and drop media onto the timeline for editing.

  • Timeline at the bottom for arranging clips
  • Preview screen for playback
  • Side panel for effects and media
  • Toolbar with essential editing tools
  • Export button in the top right corner
// Access timeline let timeline = capcut.timeline();

The toolbar includes buttons for splitting, deleting, and adjusting clip speed.

Editing Tools

CapCut offers a wide range of editing tools to enhance your videos.

Basic tools include cut, split, and reverse.

Advanced tools include chroma key, speed ramping, and motion tracking.

Text tools allow you to add dynamic and animated captions.

You can also add background music or sound effects from CapCut's library.

clip.trim(start, end) clip.speed = 2.0
Transitions and Effects

CapCut offers hundreds of built-in transitions and visual effects.

Popular transitions include fade, glitch, zoom, and spin.

Effects are categorized into video, audio, and AR filters.

You can preview and apply effects with a single click.

Effects can be adjusted in intensity and duration.

  • Fade In/Out
  • Zoom Blur
  • Retro VHS
  • Color Glitch
  • Split Screen
clip.addTransition("fade")
Exporting and Sharing

Once you're done editing, exporting your video is simple.

Choose the desired resolution and frame rate.

CapCut supports up to 4K exports for high-quality videos.

You can share directly to TikTok, YouTube, or save to your device.

Export settings include format, compression level, and watermark toggle.

project.export({ resolution: "1080p", format: "mp4" })
